Welcome to WEBDEVCON25


Where passion meets innovation

What can you expect?

WebDevCon returns to bring together IT professionals, students, and open source enthusiasts in Toronto, Ontario on May 5th - 7th, 2025. Join interactive sessions and lectures covering web design and development, cybersecurity, artifical intelligence, the developer experience, and more.

Directions

From Union Station

  1. Exit the building towards Front Street
  2. Turn left onto Front Street
  3. Walk west 450m on Front Street to the entrance of the North Building
  4. Access the SkyWalk to enter the South Building, if necessary

From Billy Bishop Airport

  1. Head northwest on Eireann Quay
  2. For the South Building, turn east on Lakeshore Blvd. W to Rees St.
  3. Continue onto Bathurst St.
  4. Turn right onto Lakeshore Blvd. W.
  5. Continue straight to stay on Lakeshore Blvd. W.
  6. Slight left toward Rees St.
  7. Use any lane to turn left onto Rees St.
  8. Turn right onto Bremner Blvd.
  9. Turn left, and the Centre will be on the right
